
---
## Ingredients

| Quantity | Measure | Ingredient |
| -------- | ------- | ---------- |
| 1        | tbs     | Honey      |
| 1/2      |         | Lemon      |
| 4        | tbs     | Butter     |

---
## Steps

- In a small saucepan melt together the butter and honey over low heat
- Squeeze the lemon into the honey mixture
- Remove from the heat
